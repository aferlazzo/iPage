Thanks for Stopping By

These pages are specifically targeted to job recruiters, as well as potential employers. You have one or more projects you need need completing. I'm searching for my next assignment. Here's a little bit about me.

You're probably thinking, "Who is this guy?" In short, I'm an excellent worker who gets the job done. I'm a highly skilled and efficient worker. I'm funny when not focused on solving a problem. I'm many things, just don't expect me to be a mind-reader. Don't expect me to somehow magically know what you want. Communication is 2-way street.

Want a team player? That's me. Nobody wins if the team as a whole isn't successful. This isn't a competition among co-workers. It's a collaboration among team-mates.

I'd like to work as close to my home as possible (I live in Pleasanton, CA). If it isn't possible, being able to use mass transit works better for me than driving myself.

Recruiters

  Dream a Little Dream

Let's talk bottom line. Wouldn't it be nice if all your clients were as easy to present as me?

Great track record. Well qualified. Seasoned. With a degree. Plays well with others, yet can think for himself.

When I don't have a keyboard in front of me for more than 20 minutes I begin to go into withdrawal.

I program in my sleep and dream of possible new UI skins for web applications.

For fun I thumb through my repeatedly studied ebook of "JavaScript: The Good Parts". It only takes a page or so to keep me thoughtfully entertained for hours.

For pleasure I download and view new talks from recent talks from CSS3 seminars posted on YouTube.

I follow industry luminaries on Twitter and subscribe to RSS feeds to kep up with news, trends, and best practices.

Employers

  Need Someone To Love?

You will love my work ethic. What employer wouldn't? Need someone that can hit the ground running, join a team, tear into a project and be productive from day one?

Looking or someone who you can provide minimum direction yet feel confident a task will get done?

That's me.

Need to help fill out your volleyball or basketball team, or a fourth for a round of golf?

In that case I can't help you.

Are you located in the East Bay? Great! Many locations in the South Bay? Still doable. The peninsula or NorthBay? Not so good. The worst part of my day would be sitting in commute traffic or in a crowded Bart train, so I want to avoid that.

Have a work-from-home option? That is something we should explore. Let's be practical and use technology like Skype or GoToMeeting for our daily stand-ups.
